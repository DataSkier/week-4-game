Pirate Booty Collector

Yar matey, this be how you play the game.

Select the treasure chest by clicking on their image. Each chest has a hidden value. On each click the booty value will be added to your total. Try to match your total booty to the goal value displayed. If you go over, walk the plank. If you can match the score you find your treasure!
